Welcome

CityTrans is a private transport company which manages inter-city transport system in the Accra. We operate in the areas of bookings, rentals, bus and subway scheduling.

The strategy is to develop excellent customer creating a timely transport network within the city, hiring the best drivers, ensuring passenger safety and applying competitive pricing structure serving all client within Accra.

We specialize promoting Ghanaian tourism by partnering with Tour companies to provide valuable experiences of the Ghanaian culture for our users. We also run mass vehicle rental services to institutions and private bodies.
